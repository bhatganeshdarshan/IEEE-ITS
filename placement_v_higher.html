About Event

The event, which started promptly at 11 am, boasted an impressive attendance of 120 students. Shreya, serving as the IEEE Student Branch Chair, kicked off the proceedings by introducing the esteemed guests and outlining the seminar's objectives. Tejas, holding the position of ITS Chapter Chair, skillfully moderated the event, fostering engagement through insightful questions and light-hearted banter. A panel of distinguished speakers graced the event, each bringing unique insights into the post-graduation landscape. Asif Ali, Gaurav Sethia, Gehna Jain, Siddharth Ashok Kumar, and Sriprasad delved into the intricacies of two prominent career paths: securing placements and pursuing higher studies. They shared personal anecdotes, highlighting the pros and cons of each path while emphasizing the importance of aligning choices with individual passions and long-term goals. Throughout the seminar, attendees were treated to invaluable advice on excelling in interviews and navigating the professional sphere with confidence. The speakers encouraged students to explore diverse interests and embrace opportunities for growth, reassuring them that mistakes are integral to the learning journey. The event served as more than just a platform for career guidance; it fostered a sense of mentorship and camaraderie between the guests and students. Attendees eagerly absorbed wisdom from the accomplished alumni, finding inspiration to carve their own paths in the professional world. As the event drew to a close, Thrivedhi, serving as Joint Secretary, expressed gratitude to the guests for their enlightening contributions. Backstage, heartfelt words of appreciation were exchanged, and memorable moments were captured in photographs, marking the culmination of a truly impactful event. In conclusion, the seminar left a lasting impression on attendees, equipping them with the knowledge and confidence to make informed career decisions. It underscored the importance of seeking guidance and mentorship while reinforcing the notion that success lies in pursuing one's passions with determination and resilience. The event not only enriched students' understanding of post-graduation options but also served as a beacon of inspiration within the IEEE community of BMSIT&M.
